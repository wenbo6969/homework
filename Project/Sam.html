Description


Sam is a damage dealer and has the rarity of chromatic, he is a close range brawler. His appareance is a old men with metal claws as hands. His attacks are named heavy haymakers and super, knuckle busters. Heavy haymaker make it that he punches with his claws, meanwhile his super makes him throw a giant claw at people. Now, Sam has this unique ability that he begins the game with his super and after throwing it, can pick it back up which will instantely charge it up again. He also gains a speed boost everytime he throws it.
